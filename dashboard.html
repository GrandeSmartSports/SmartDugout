#002: Owner-Delegate Workflow

Category: Process Management

Decision: Implemented a hands-off documentation system where the owner only gives two commands: "Review the Project Hub" to start sessions and "Update our Project Hub with what we discussed" to end them.

Impact: Owner workload reduced to just two commands, Claude instances fully autonomous in documentation.
